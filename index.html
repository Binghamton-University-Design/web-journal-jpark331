Project 1

A Visual Message for the Inhabitants of Other Celestial Bodies

This visual message is an open invitation to extraterrestrials. Several assumptions were made prior to the creation of this image. First, the observable laws of the universe apply in all parts of the universe. Second, extraterrestrials with higher levels of intelligence and awareness exist. And finally, all lifeforms with higher levels of intelligence develop an understanding of mathematics.

The first component of the message marks location by emulating the structure of our solar system, which serves as a form of landmark. Although it may be difficult to determine location at a distance, it is doubtful that this message will travel far enough to elicit confusion.

The second component of the message identifies the senders. The most basic form of communication is body language, which is why diagrams are provided of the most simplest behaviors communicating peace. To demonstrate range of motion, the body is simplified into simple shapes while the joints are emphasized.

The third component of the message is a visual representation of the Fibonacci sequence, which appears sporadically in nature and could be defined as the mathematical equivalent of planet Earth. If not, it could at least mark similarities between us and extraterrestrials, opening new avenues of communication.
