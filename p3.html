JIN PARK | WEB JOURNAL | CHAPTER 3

Project 3

UI Design Process, Person, Place, Thing

I made a website about NFTs, a relatively new development in the world of cryptocurrency and digital art. There are a lot of misconceptions about NFTs and cryptocurrency, especially through the lens of greed.

The first page is an about page, detailing everything one would need to know about NFTs. Its utility for artists is significant, allowing a digital market for art to exist.

The second page is a timeline, allowing people to expand on their research or get introduced to new concepts. NFTs have allowed people to pioneer a lot of firsts, such as the sale of the first tweet ever by Jack Dorsey.

Finally, the third page is a blog where my opinions on cryptocurrency and NFTs are consolidated. This is the part that allows me to tell people what I believe in, why I decided to invest in crypto, and what the future holds for NFTs.
