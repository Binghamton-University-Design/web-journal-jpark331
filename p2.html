JIN PARK | WEB JOURNAL | CHAPTER 2

Project 2

Emergent Interaction, Exquisite Corpse

For the next chapter in my web journal, Brian Wissing and I participated in the surrealist activity known as the Exquisite Corpse. This abstract amalgamation of our collective graphics may have arbitrary connections between each image, but we believe that as a whole, every part of the "body" is accounted for. To initiate and inspire our work, we established the following rules:

1. Subject matter is limited only by imagination

2. Could be an image, sketch, or a product of Photoshop/Illustrator

3. Brian's additions must be brightly themed

4. Jin's additions must be darkly themed

5. 3x3 square with 9 separate parts

6. Brian begins on the top left, I follow with the top middle, and we both continue to alternate in a clockwise spiral direction.
